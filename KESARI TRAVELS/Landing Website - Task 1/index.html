NIAGARA FALLS

Niagara Country

Niagara Falls consists of two waterfalls on the Niagara River, which marks the border between New York and Ontario, Canada: the American Falls, located on the American side of the border,and the Canadian or Horseshoe Falls located on the Canadian side.

All inclusive price Starts From Rs.100,938

EMPIRE STATE BUILDING

Midtown Manhattan

With its soaring height and signature design, the Empire State Building defines the New York City skyline. The world’s most magnificent Art Deco skyscraper, it’s a living piece of New York history and an instantly recognizable symbol of city culture today.

All inclusive price Starts From Rs.85,998

BROOKLYN BRIDGE

East River

The Brooklyn Bridge is a hybrid cable-stayed/suspension bridge in New York City, spanning the East River between the boroughs of Manhattan and Brooklyn. Opened on May 24, 1883, the Brooklyn Bridge was the first fixed crossing of the East River.

All inclusive price Starts From Rs.100,938

LAKE GEORGE

Adirondack region

Lake George is located at the southern end of the famed Adirondack Park in Upstate New York.It is considered to be among the clearest and cleanest large lakes in the world.Lake George is a headwater lake, fed by dozens of mountain streams and dotted with more than 170 islands of varying shapes and sizes.Lake George serves as the primary source of drinking water for surrounding communities and residents.

TIMES SQUARE

Manhattan,NEW YORK

Times Square is a major commercial intersection, tourist destination, entertainment hub, and neighborhood in Midtown Manhattan, Times Square is a bowtie-shaped plaza five blocks long between 42nd and 47th Streets.

All inclusive price Starts From Rs.125,000

ROCKEFELLER CENTER

Midtown Manhattan

Rockefeller Center is a large complex consisting of 19 commercial buildings covering 22 acres (89,000 m2) between 48th Street and 51st Street in the Midtown Manhattan neighborhood of New York City designed by a team of architects headed by Henry Hofmeister, H.W. Corbett, and Raymond Hood.

Continue Reading

ELLIS ISLAND

b/w NEW JERSEY & NEW YORK

Ellis Island, island in Upper New York Bay, formerly the United States’ principal immigration reception centre. Often referred to as the Gateway to the New World, the island lies about 1 mile (1.6 km).It is part of the Statue of Liberty National Monument and is accessible to the public only by ferry.

All inclusive price Starts From Rs.75,999

THE BRONX ZOO

Bronx

The Bronx Zoo is a zoo within Bronx Park in the Bronx, New York. It is one of the largest zoos in the United States by area and is the largest metropolitan zoo in the United States by area, comprising 265 acres of park lands and naturalistic habitats separated by the Bronx River.The Bronx Zoo is one of the finest in the world with over 5,000 animals of more than 700 species.

All inclusive price Starts From Rs.50,000

THE CATSKILL MOUNTAINS

Greene & Ulster Countries,Southern NYC

Catskill Mountains, dissected segment of the Allegheny Plateau, part of the Appalachian Mountain system, lying mainly in Greene and Ulster counties, southeastern New York, U.S. Bounded north and east by the valleys of the Mohawk and Hudson rivers, respectively, the mountains are drained by headstreams of the Delaware River and by numerous small creeks.

All inclusive price Starts From Rs.65,232

CENTRAL PARK

Midtown Manhattan

Central Park is an urban park between the Upper West Side and Upper East Side neighborhoods of Manhattan in New York City, United States. It is the fifth-largest park in the city, containing 843 acres (341 ha), and the most visited urban park in the United States.

All inclusive price Starts From Rs.128,938

THE METROPOLITAN MUSEUM OF ART

New York City, NY

The Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York City, colloquially "the Met", is the largest art museum in the Americas. In 2022 it welcomed 3,208,832 visitors, ranking it the third most visited U.S museum, and eighth on the list of most-visited art museums in the world. Its permanent collection contains over two million works, divided among 17 curatorial departments.

All inclusive price Starts From Rs.200,878

THE HIGH LINE

Manhattan West Side

The High Line is an elevated freight rail line transformed into a public park on Manhattan’s West Side. It is owned by the City of New York, and maintained and operated by Friends of the High Line. Founded in 1999 by community residents, Friends of the High Line fought for the High Line’s preservation and transformation at a time when the historic structure was under the threat of demolition.
